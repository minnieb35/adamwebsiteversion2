Spotlight

The artists I regularly work with are so wonderfully talented I felt the need to feature them here. Join me in celebrating them!

Iconic Bond
Ahmed Radwan is a multifaceted individual who wears many hats with remarkable skill. Co-founder and CEO of The House of Creatives, Ahmed is also widely recognized as Iconic Bond, an Egyptian-American Hip-Hop Artist.
Zack Roberson
It all started with an invitation to play at George Clinton's birthday party on July 22, 1998. Zack "#9" Roberson began an amazing trip on the stage of the Lion's Den, in the East Village of New York City. In 2023, Zack Roberson and his Nappy Head Funk Army was officially inducted into the Funk Hall of Fame in recognition for their ground-setting and continued work to be ambassadors of a true form of black American music around the world.
Flower in Bloom
Flower in Bloom is a rising alternative R&B artist based in Winston-Salem, NC. With the fluidity and freedom her unique style embodies, Flower makes the kind of music that feels right to her in the moment which enables her to connect with her audience in new and authentic ways.
Naomi G Bennett
Naomi G Bennett is a folk singer-songwriter who writes about her experiences with love, pain and hardships. Her lyrics are woven with depth and emotion as she sings about past experiences and trauma.
Donovan Harrell
Singing for over 12 years, Donovan started out singing at the age of 7 in his church choir and then went on to sing in local talent shows and open mics around Raleigh. He has worked with the likes of Skyblew, Jamaal Matters and Toni Contini.
Sintead
Sintead is a psychedelic indie washed in dreamy vocal harmonies and punctuated by driving percussion. Influenced by Bloc Party, Melody's Echo Chamber, Club of the Sons, Vinyl Williams, Mutemath, and Chris Cohen.
